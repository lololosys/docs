## Add Teammates

Invite your team members to collaborate on your Docswrite workspace.

Once you add a teammate, they will be able to access the sites in this organization. Ask them to log in with their email address.
You will need to add your teammate's email address, the role (admin or collaborator)

## Organization Details

You can update your organization details at any time.

**Organization Name**
_This is the name that will be displayed on your invoices. You can change it as needed._

**Billing Address**
_This is the address we will use for billing. You may update it if your billing information changes._
