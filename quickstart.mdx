## Getting Started with Docswrite

Welcome to Docswrite! Follow these simple steps to connect your accounts and start publishing Google Docs to WordPress with just one click.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect Google Drive Account">
    Once you login to Docswrite, you will be asked to connect your Google Drive.

    ### Step 1: Click on the "Connect Google Drive" button

    ![google-drive-connect](/images/google-drive-connect.png)

    ### Step 2: Select your Google account

    ![Select Google Account](/images/select-google-account.png)

    ### Step 3: Click on the "Select All" button and "Continue" button

    ![Select All](/images/approve-google.png)

    Once you have connected your Google Drive, you can access all your Google Docs from the Docswrite dashboard.

  </Step>

  <Step title="Connect WordPress Website">
    After connecting your Google Drive, you'll need to connect your WordPress site to enable publishing.

    ### WordPress Connection Options

    You can connect your WordPress site using:
    - **WordPress.com hosted sites** - Direct connection
    - **Self-hosted WordPress** - Using application passwords or our WordPress plugin
    - **WordPress plugin** - Enhanced features and better compatibility

    For detailed WordPress connection instructions, see our [WordPress Connection Guide](/essentials/wordpress-connection).

  </Step>

  <Step title="Start Publishing">
    With both accounts connected, you're ready to publish! You can now:

    - **Dashboard Flow**: Use our clean dashboard for one-click publishing
    - **API Integration**: Build custom workflows with our REST API
    - **Zapier Automation**: Connect to your existing content calendar
    - **Third-party Tools**: Integrate with ClickUp, Trello, and more

    ### Your First Publication

    1. Create or open a Google Doc
    2. Make sure it's shared with "Anyone with the link"
    3. Go to your Docswrite dashboard
    4. Select the document and configure your publishing settings
    5. Click "Publish to WordPress"

    That's it! Your content will be published to WordPress with all formatting preserved.

  </Step>
</Steps>

## Troubleshooting

Having issues? Check these common solutions:

- **Google Drive not connecting**: Make sure you're selecting all permissions when authorizing
- **WordPress connection failed**: Verify your site URL and credentials
- **Document not found**: Ensure your Google Doc is shared with "Anyone with the link"
- **Publishing failed**: Check that your WordPress site is accessible and properly connected

For more help, visit our [Support page](/support) or check our [FAQs](/essentials/faqs).
